Information

Access

Access to pedestrian gate, buildings, bedrooms and social rooms are controlled by your Swipe Card issued upon arrival.

Access for visitors is strictly controlled and between 09:00hrs and 22:00hrs. All visitors must report to Reception.

Activites

Please view Activities Noticeboard in Reception and also in Seomra Failte regarding on and off site activities.

Addresss

Balseskin Reception Centre, St Margarets Road, Finglas, Dublin 11. Eircode D11 FX93 Tel 01 8646291

Bed Linen

Bed Linen is provided on arrival by Reception.

Should you require additional linen, please contact Reception.

Dirty Bedlinen can be exchanged at reception.

Bedrooms

Entry to bedrooms is by use of swipe cards.

Bedrooms must be kept clean and tidy, cleaning equipment and materials are available at the Cleaning Station and Reception.

Smoking is NOT PERMITTED inside Balseskin.

A notice in your bedroom gives directions in the event of a fire.

If you notice a maintenance issue in your bedroom/bathroom please contact Reception and we will do our utmost to rectify it.

If anything in your bedroom/bathroom or around the Centre is broken please let us know and we will get our Maintenance Personnel to fix it.

Baby

Baby Cots/Baskets are available on request at Reception.

Baby milk and Bottle Sterilizer are available at reception

Baby food is available at the Dining Area.

Nappies are available at Reception until you receive Social Welfare.

Bus Services

Balseskin offer a free bus service into Mountjoy Square. Bus timetables are available at Reception. Tickets will be given out 45 minutes prior to the buses departure time.

Public buses (83 & 40B) also run from the local bus stop to the city centre from 7am-11pm.

Catering/Canteen

Meal Times

Breakfast: 08:00 to 10:00

Lunch: 12:30 to 14:00

Dinner: 17:00 to 18:30

If you have a special dietary need (ie. you are a diabetic, etc.) please contact a member of the catering staff who will cater for your special needs.

Cleaning

Parents are responsible for the care, safety and behaviour of their children at all times.

Parents are not permitted to leave children alone in the Centre at any time when they go out, for whatever reason. Proper arrangements must be in place for their care. The Centre Staff/Management must be informed, and be satisfied with such arrangements.

Please read the Child Protection Notice Board in the admin building.

Balseskin have an onsite Supervised Play room, please contact staff on site.

Fire Safety

A continuous siren alarm is the signal to evacuate the Building.

Fire instructions are posted in each bedroom and throughout the Centre. These instructions are to help you to understand what to do in the event of a fire

If the alarm sounds, leave the building and go to your assembly point and wait there until you are told to do otherwise.

Do not collect personal belongings.

Please keep fire exits clear at all time.

Fire Drills will be held on a regular basis at the Centre â€“ Please assist where possible.

If you discover a fire, please activate the nearest fire alarm call point and then leave the building immediately. DO NOT TRY AND TACKLE THE FIRE YOURSELF.

You are required to co-operate with the management of the centre in the evacuation of the premises in the event of a fire or a fire drill. Failure to co-operate will be considered a serious breach of House Rules and the Reception and Integration Agency will be notified.

First Aid

First Aid is available from Reception.

All treatments or use of first aid box must be reported to a member of Staff/Management.

In an emergency contact Reception or any member of Staff/Management

Balseskin Medical Centre is opened Monday to Friday.

Laundry Room

Washing machines and dryers are available for your use in the laundry room.

Iron and ironing boards are available from Reception

Washing tablets can be collected from the Reception office

Letter/Parcels

Letters, parcels, etc will be available for collection at Reception.

There is a Post Box at Reception for outgoing post.

Medical

Balseskin Reception Centre have a Medical Centre/Social Worker onsite. For out of hour service please contact Reception where staff can organise appropriate transport to hospital.

Shopping

Charlestown Shopping Centre in 10 minute walk from Balseskin, please ask at Reception for directions.

Toiletries

Toilet paper, small soap, and shampoo are available at Reception when required.

Visitors

Visitors are only allowed on site (Admin Building) as follows:
Adults: 09am to 10pm
Children: 09am to 6pm

Each visitor must sign in at Reception and adhere to the house rules.
